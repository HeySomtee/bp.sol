How To Buy

Download Phantom Wallet

Get your free Phantom Wallet from either the App Store or Google Play Store, or visit phantom.app to download the extension for your browser. This will be your gateway to managing and trading SOL and $BP.

Buy Solana on a CEX

To acquire $BP, first ensure you have SOL in your wallet. You can purchase SOL on various centralized exchanges (CEX) like Binance, Coinbase, or Kraken. Once you have SOL, transfer it to your Phantom Wallet.

Go to Raydium

Access Raydium through your preferred browser by visiting raydium.io. Connect your Phantom Wallet, enter the $BP token address on Raydium, and select $BP for trading. Confirm the transaction through your Phantom Wallet to proceed.

Swap SOL for $BP

Trade your SOL for $BP effortlessly on Raydium. Enjoy the seamless transaction process, and adjust slippage if necessary during high volatility to ensure smooth trading.
